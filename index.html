Delivery for the on-demand era

Revolutionising the future of urban logistics, Stuart is the leading on-demand solution powering the way goods are transported in a customised way. We connect businesses across all industries and of all sizes to high quality independent couriers to offer customised delivery solutions to wow their end customers.

Speed and Simplicity in just a few Clicks

Cross-Vertical Délivery

Our platform can help you manage all of your last mile needs. We work with restaurants, grocers, retail, e-commerce and offer a variety of delivery services, from on-demand to scheduled deliveries.

Ship from Anywhere

We can ship your goods from whichever urban location is most convenient for you: from your store, from an urban warehouse or from our very own city hub.

Advanced Technology

Thanks to our cutting edge technology you can track your deliveries in real time, from pick up to drop off, and can share this with your end-customer through an automated messaging service.

Multi-Modal Fleet

We connect you to a fleet of independent couriers, who will be able to complete your deliveries on the appropriate transport type for your package size, including bikes, mopeds, e-scooters and cars
